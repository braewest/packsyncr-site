A free, user-friendly service for sharing and collaborating on Minecraft resource packs

Overview

packsyncr is a simple and secure platform for sharing and collaborating on Minecraft resource packs. It keeps packs up to date automatically, letting you collaborate seamlessly
without manual downloads.

Features

Collaborative Packs

Invite your friends, teammates, or community members to collaborate on a single shared pack. Changes made by one contributor appear for everyone automatically.

Simple Sharing

Generate a sharable link or invite collaborators to your resource pack. Control their access level, whether you want public sharing, private invites, or contributor permissions.

Automatic Syncing

Every user's local files stay up to date. When you launch Minecraft, packsyncr checks for updates and ensures you always have the latest version of your packs.

Cloud Storage

All your resources are securely stored in the cloud, accessible from any device that has the permissions to view it.
